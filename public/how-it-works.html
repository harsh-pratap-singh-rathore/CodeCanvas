How CodeCanvas Works

From basic details to a complete website in three simple steps.

01

Enter Your Details

Provide basic information about yourself, your work, or your business. Include your name, role, background, skills, projects, or services. The more context you provide, the better your website will be.

02

AI Builds Your Website

CodeCanvas AI analyzes your information and generates professional content. It selects an appropriate template, structures your sections, writes clear copy, and creates a complete website layout tailored to your needs.

03

Preview & Use

Review your generated website in real-time. Make adjustments to content, swap sections, or refine details. Once satisfied, publish your website and share it with the world.

What Makes CodeCanvas Different

  • AI-generated content written specifically for you, not generic placeholders
  • Instant preview so you see exactly what you're getting
  • Clean, professional design that looks expensive
  • Mobile-friendly by default
  • No coding, no hosting setup, no technical knowledge needed
