Getting Started

Create your first website in minutes with this simple guide.

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email address. No credit card required to start. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

Choose Your Website Type

Select what kind of website you need:

  • Personal Website — Simple online presence
  • Portfolio Website — Showcase your work and skills
  • Business Landing — Professional site for your business or service

Not sure? You can browse templates first to see what fits your needs.

Provide Your Information

Fill out a simple form with relevant details:

  • Your name and role
  • Background or bio
  • Skills or services
  • Projects or experience (if applicable)
  • Contact information
  • Links to social profiles or other platforms

The more detail you provide, the better your website will be. But you can always edit and refine later.

Let AI Build Your Website

CodeCanvas AI processes your information and generates a complete website. This takes just a few seconds. The AI writes professional content, selects an appropriate template, and structures everything logically.

Review and Customize

Preview your generated website. You can:

  • Edit any text or content
  • Add or remove sections
  • Adjust layout elements
  • Change template if needed

Everything updates in real-time so you see exactly what your visitors will see.

Publish Your Website

Once you're satisfied, click publish. Your website goes live instantly at your chosen URL. You can share it immediately.

Need to make changes later? Just log in and edit. Updates publish instantly.

Create your account and build your first website now.
