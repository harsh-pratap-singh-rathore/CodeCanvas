Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about CodeCanvas and how it works.

Do I need coding skills to use CodeCanvas?
No. CodeCanvas is designed for people with no technical background. You provide information about yourself or your business, and the AI handles everything else.
How long does it take to create a website?
Most users complete their website in under 10 minutes. The AI generation itself takes only a few seconds. The rest is reviewing and making any adjustments you want.
Can I edit my website after it's created?
Yes. You can edit any content, add or remove sections, and update information at any time. Changes publish instantly.
Do I need to provide my own hosting?
No. CodeCanvas provides hosting as part of the service. Your website is hosted on fast, reliable infrastructure with no setup required.
Can I use my own domain name?
Yes. You can connect your own custom domain or use a CodeCanvas subdomain for free.
Is my website mobile-friendly?
Yes. All CodeCanvas templates are responsive and work perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktop computers.
What kind of websites can I create?
CodeCanvas is designed for personal websites, portfolios, and business landing pages. If you need a simple, professional web presence, CodeCanvas is the right tool.
How does the AI generate content?
You provide basic information in a structured form. The AI analyzes your input, identifies key points, and writes professional copy tailored to your needs. It also selects an appropriate template and organizes your content logically.
Is there a free trial?
Yes. You can create and preview your website for free. No credit card required to start.
Can I switch templates after my site is created?
Yes. You can change templates at any time. Your content will automatically adapt to the new template structure.
What if I need help?
Contact support anytime through the contact form. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.
Can I export my website?
Yes. You can download your website as static HTML files if you want to host it elsewhere.
