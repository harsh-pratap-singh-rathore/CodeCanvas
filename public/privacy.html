Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026. CodeCanvas is committed to absolute data privacy.

Data Collection under V2 Local Setup

Under the CodeCanvas v2 architecture, you have the option to process language generation locally using Ollama. In local mode, zero data from your portfolio generation prompts is sent to CodeCanvas servers.

Account Information

When you sign up via Email or Google OAuth, we collect basic profile metadata (Name, Email Address) necessary to securely persist your saved portfolio blueprints to our database.

Analytics and Cookies

We use strict minimal cookies exclusively for session authentication. We do not embed 3rd-party tracking telemetry or advertisement tracking pixels into your generated portfolios or our dashboard infrastructure.

Your Rights

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated JSON blueprints at any point by contacting us.
