## 🎯 Key Features Implemented

### Navigation System
- **Non-fixed header** (scrolls naturally with page)
- **Click-to-open dropdowns** for Solutions and Help & Resources
- **Click outside to close** functionality
- **Escape key support** to close dropdowns
- **Smooth animations** (opacity + translateY)

### Design Philosophy Adherence
- ✅ Pure white background (#FFFFFF)
- ✅ Near-black text (#0F0F0F)
- ✅ Secondary gray (#6B6B6B)
- ✅ Black accent only
- ✅ No gradients, no neon, no dark theme
- ✅ System fonts (Inter, SF Pro, Segoe UI)
- ✅ Flat UI with 1px light borders
- ✅ 4px border radius
- ✅ Generous white space
- ✅ Maximum restraint

### Content Quality
- ✅ All pages have **real, meaningful content**
- ✅ No lorem ipsum or placeholder text
- ✅ Clear value propositions
- ✅ Practical information
- ✅ Professional copywriting

### Responsive Design
